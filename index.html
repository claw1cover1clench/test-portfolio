About Me

I'm a passionate AI researcher and photographer with over 5 years of experience in creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between technology and art. My work focuses on developing AI systems that can understand and create visual content, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in creative technology.

When I'm not debugging neural networks, you can find me exploring the world with my camera, capturing moments that tell stories. I believe that both AI and photography are about seeing patterns and creating meaningful connections between elements.

What I'm Working On

Currently, I'm developing an AI-powered photography assistant that helps users compose better shots in real-time. This project combines my expertise in computer vision with my passion for photography, creating tools that enhance rather than replace human creativity.
